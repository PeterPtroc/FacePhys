FacePhys Demo V1.0

FacePhys leverages State Space Models (SSMs) to model heart dynamics as continuous-time controlled ODEs. This ensures robust handling of irregular sampling and precise capture of physiological signals.

Optimized for the edge inference, the model is ultra-lightweight (4MB) with latency as low as 5ms.

Powered by LiteRT, inference runs in your browser. Privacy is guaranteed with zero data upload.
